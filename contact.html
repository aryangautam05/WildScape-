Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions to help you learn more about our services and policies.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the New York City metropolitan area but offer consultation and remote support for select nearby regions. Contact us to check availability in your location.

How can I schedule a garden consultation?

You can schedule consultations by filling the contact form or calling our office during business hours. We will arrange a site visit at a convenient time.

What smart irrigation systems do you install?

We install customized drip and sprinkler systems with integrated soil moisture sensors and smart controllers compatible with mobile apps for real-time monitoring.

Can I upgrade my existing irrigation system?

Yes, we offer retrofit services to upgrade traditional irrigation systems to smart, water-efficient technology tailored to your garden.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Our maintenance packages include seasonal check-ups, system calibration, repairs, and support services to keep your garden’s irrigation optimal.

What makes a garden eco-friendly?

Using native plants, organic soil amendments, sustainable watering, and promoting biodiversity characterize an eco-friendly garden.

How long does installation take?

Installation timelines vary based on garden size and complexity but typically range from 1 to 5 days including setup and initial monitoring calibration.

Are your services covered by warranty?

Yes, we provide warranties on installation workmanship and equipment as stated in service agreements.

How do I monitor my irrigation system remotely?

Our systems feature app integration that provides alerts, watering schedules, and control from your smartphone or desktop.

What if I have an emergency with my irrigation system?

Contact our support line immediately. We provide emergency repair services to prevent water damage or prolonged outages.
